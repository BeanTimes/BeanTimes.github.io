April 10th, 1588.

To those who have survived: I write this now, for I fear my death is imminent. I originally wrote this journal to catalog information about the Beans in hopes of finding a way to defeat them, or at the very least, put up the slightest semblance of a fight as we were mercilessly slaughtered. All of my collected information about the different types of Beans and their abilities are detailed later within this journal. Do not let us die. Not like this.
