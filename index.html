First Website Test and Ad Test.

November 5th, 2020

This is a website designed for testing purposes! I'm sending this to you so you can see how the content looks on the beta version of the website design without actually posting all the stuff online. This is the "News" section, where news will be displayed. The news will follow this general setup with title, date, and content. The words on the top are links to other parts of the site. The patreon link will link to our patreon once we create it. The table of contents works, and the about tab will be information about the project and us. I'm thinking about adding cookie support to save and load your position in the book, and maybe some different backgrounds depending on time of day of the chapter (or maybe even real life idk). If you look at the black background, it's stars rn. Anyways, click the big start reading button or go to the table of contents to see the actual stuff.
