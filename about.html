Welcome to the Bean Times

In the year 1587, an army of human corpses controlled by layers of sentient baked beans attacks the Holy Roman Empire, throwing humanity into a struggle for survival.

The Bean Times is a story written and published by a team of two. Updates will be posted every Friday at 11 PM Eastern, 8 PM Pacific.
